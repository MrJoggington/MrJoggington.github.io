About Me

My name is Ghett Lee, and I am an anthropology student of three years. I plan to finish my B.A. by 2020, and do amazing things with the power of qualtitative research. There is no shortage of questions to why I chose this path, but there is no shortage of answers as well.

The profession itself may be found lacking in careers that reach economic solitude, but what it lacks, it makes up for with its broad analytical values; it's multiple angles and open minds.

Anthropology has stood the test of time, and has constantly evolved as humans have for thousands years, the future is merely a mystery, where to others it is a means of an end. I am proud of my domain, and I wouldn't have it any other way.
